My idea is to create a website where every day you can buy a different LEGO figurine. It's not about the same figurines that LEGO is selling and millions of other people already have, but those figurines will have a unique design created by LEGO fans for LEGO fans. Designed projects will be printed on the clean, plain figurines using the same technique that is used for the original figurines - a pad printing technique. In this way the quality will be similar to the quality of figures purchased in the store. Projects submitted by designers figures will be judged by others (anyone can visit the website and vote for a design), then every day a new figurine will be selected. Then you can buy it only in next 24 hours.

How am I going to get designs ? It's simple - authors of the best figurines (those that will be sold) will receive, in addition to some free figures, part of the profits from the sale. It will probably be about 10% of the profits from each figure - the amount may be slightly less if my calculations of the printer and the materials costs are too optimistic. What happens to the rest of the money? First of all, most of them will go to pay off the printer (printer for pad printing costs about 10 000 $), materials (the cost of one container of paint is 50 $), plain figurines on which I will print and other costs that will surely emerge in the later phase.

I will add pictures and describe the fundrising purpose soon !
